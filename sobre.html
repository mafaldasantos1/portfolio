About me

Hello! I'm Mafalda and I am a Digital Product Designer. As a person, I am an organized, practical, communicative and curious person. My curiosity drives me to constantly explore new ideas and technologies, always looking for ways to improve my craft and create functional and innovative products.

I have a bachelor's degree in Graphic and Multimedia Design and a specialization in Digital Experience Design, which I got through the Post-Graduation from the Faculty of Fine Arts - University of Lisbon course in 2023. I also did the Erasmus+ Programme at Escola Massana in Barcelona, in the first semester of the last year of my bachelor's degree.

I am excited about opportunities to contribute my design expertise and collaborate with others to create impactful products. I am always looking forward to growing as a person and as a professional.
